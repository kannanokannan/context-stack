# Family Doctrine

Probabilistic intelligence must operate inside deterministic governance boundaries.

Across the stack:

- LLMs may interpret, extract, and narrate.
- Deterministic systems validate, compute, authorize, and record side effects.
- Missing permission is not permission.
- Governance remains the authority layer.

This doctrine governs ContextOps, ContextBoundary, Sthala, and Griha.
