# ContextBoundary

ContextBoundary answers: where is data allowed to go?

It owns:

- Egress Tier definitions
- vendor zones
- jurisdictional audit profiles
- data movement policy
- ContextOps bridge mapping

ContextBoundary is deployment-agnostic. It governs egress regardless of where AI runs.
